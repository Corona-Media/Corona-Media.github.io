20+ Great Books For Kids To Read While Stuck At Home




Bored? Need a new book to read? Try out these awesome kid's books hand-picked by other children also stuck at home. Reading is both fun and educational, but it's even better with a friend. We suggest starting a book club with friends and family to help keep the boredom at bay. Have anything you want to add to this list?
