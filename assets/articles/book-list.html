20+ Great Books For Kids To Read While Stuck At Home




Bored? Out of books to read? Try out these suggestions hand picked by other kids. You could read them on your own or start a book club with your family or friends. Have anything you want to add to this list? Email us at Info@CovidKidsOnline.com. Happy reading!



  • The Land Of Stories series by Chris Colfer
  • A Tale Of Magic Series by Chris Colfer
  • Fablehaven series by Brandon Mull
  • The Menagerie series by Tui T. Sutherland
  • 13 Secrets series Michelle Harrison
  • Panda-monium by Stewart Gibbs
  • Aru Shah and the End of Time series by Roshani Chokshi
  • The Mysterious Benedict Society by Trenton Lee Stewart
  • Brazen: Rebel Ladies Who Rocked the World by Penelope Bagieu
  • Olympians series by George O'Conner
  • Percy Jackson series by Rick Riordan
  • Kane Chronicles by Rick Riordan
  • The Apothecary by Maile Meloy
  • Children of Blood and Bone by Toni Adeyemi
  • Evil Genius by Catherine Jinks
  • The Blackthorn Key by Kevin Sands
  • The Hobbit by J.R.R. Tolkein
  • Fahrenheit 51 by Ray Bradbury
  • The Book Thief by Markus Zusak
  • Ready Player One by Ernest Cline
  • The London Eye Mystery by Siobhan Dowd

Have some suggestions of your own? Email us at Info@CovidKidsOnline.com.
