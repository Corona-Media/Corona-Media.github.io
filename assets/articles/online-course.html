The 3 Best Online Course Sites




Not sure how to plan your summer? Taking an online course is a great way to pass the time and learn a new skill. Here we will list the best online coures sites to keep you busy and learning over the summer. Happy Learning!


1. Edx

Edx is a great website to learn almost anything. They have a huge variety of online courses in every subject.


2. Khan Academy

Khan Academy is another great platform for learning. Most of it's courses are around topics covered in school like math and science.


3. Coursera

Coursera also has a wide range of courses including computer science and history.
