50+ Activities to keep you busy while bored at home




Bored? Need some new activities to pass the time? Try out these ideas from other kids also stuck at home. Have an idea you want to add? Email us at Info@CovidKidsOnline.com.



  • Play Video Games
  • Listen To Music
  • Learn A Musical Instrument
  • Watch Youtube
  • Watch A Movie Or TV Show
  • Play A Board Or Card Game
  • Read The News
  • Do Extra Chores
  • Learn To Code
  • Take An Online Course
  • Write A Story
  • Write A Journal Or Diary
  • Build A Pillow Fort
  • Build A Cardboard Box Fort
  • Go On A Bike Ride, Run, Or Walk
  • Start A Garden
  • Do A Puzzle
  • Organize Your Books By Color
  • Draw Or Color
  • Make A Collage With Scraps Of Paper
  • Start A Vlog Or Blog About your Time Stuck At Home
  • Write A Song
  • Make Something Out Of Clay
  • Paint A Picture
  • Listen To A Podcast
  • Start Learning A New Language
  • Call, Text, Or Email A friend
  • Read A Book Or Ebook
  • Make A Comic Book
  • Start A Book Club With Family Or Friends
  • Paint Rocks
  • Make Homemade Playdough Or Slime
  • Play Multiplayer Video Games With Your Friends
  • Video Chat With Friends
  • Bake A Cake Or Cupcakes
  • Perfect A Recipe
  • Make Breakfast For Dinner
  • Try Out A Mini Baking Kit
  • Play Solitare
  • Play Uno
  • Play Madlibs
  • Make Something With Lego
  • Play A Game Of Chess
  • Play With Your Pet
  • Read Outside
  • Take A Virtual Exercise Class
  • Take A Virtual Museum Tour
