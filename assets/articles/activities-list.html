30+ Activities to keep you busy while bored at home




Bored? Need some new activities to pass the time? Try out these ideas from other kids also stuck at home. Have an idea you want to add? Email us at Info@CovidKidsOnline.com.



  • Read A Book
  • Play Video Games
  • Call or Text A friend
  • Listen To Music
  • Learn A Musical Instrument
  • Watch Youtube
  • Watch A Movie Or TV Show
  • Play A Board Or Card Game
  • Read The News
  • Learn A New Language
  • Do Extra Chores
  • Learn To Code
  • Take An Online Course
  • Write A Story
  • Write A Journal Or Diary
  • Build A Pillow Fort
  • Build A Cardboard Box Fort
  • Go On A Bike Ride, Run, Or Walk
  • Start A Garden
  • Do A Puzzle
  • Organize Your Books By Color
  • Bake
  • Cook
  • Draw Or Color
  • Make A Collage With Scraps Of Paper
  • Start A Vlog Or Blog About your Time Stuck At Home
  • Write A Song
  • Make Something Out Of Clay
  • Paint A Picture
  • Listen To A Podcast

Are we missing something? Email us at Info@CovidKidsOnline.com to suggest additional activities.
